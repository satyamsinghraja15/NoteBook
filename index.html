Satyam Singh Raja

Main is chhoti si khat mein apne dil ki baatein likh raha hoon, jo main hamesha kehna chahta tha. Tum meri zindagi ki sabse mahatvapurna hissa ho, aur main har din dhanyavad deta hoon ki tum mere saath ho.

Tumhara saath mere liye ek anmol uphaar hai. Tumhari muskurahat, tumhari baatein, aur tumhara pyaar meri zindagi ko khushnuma banate hain. Tumhara pyaar mere liye ek jyot hai, jo meri andheri zindagi mein ujala bhar deta hai.

Main jaanta hoon ki humare beech kabhi-kabhi mushkilein aati hain, lekin jab bhi woh aati hain, mujhe yaad dilata hai ki humare pyaar mein takat hai. Tumhara saath mere liye sab kuch hai, aur main hamesha tumhare saath rahunga.

Tum meri premika ho, lekin tum meri saathi, meri dost, aur meri sahara bhi ho. Tumhara saath mere liye anmol hai.

Main tumse itna pyaar karta hoon ki shabdon mein vyakt kar pana mushkil hai, lekin main hamesha yahi kehna chahta hoon - Tum meri zindagi ki sabse khoobsurat aur pyaari insaan ho. Tumhara saath pane ke liye main hamesha aabhari rahunga.

Tumhara Sattuuu
